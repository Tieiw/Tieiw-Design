We are a design studio dedicated to designing wedding and party products, with over 1000 square meters of space and 5 professional designers. Our products are sold in various countries, including the United States, Spain, and France.

In our studio, we put customers at the center and innovation at the core, constantly pursuing excellent design and high-quality products. Our design team has years of experience and an in-depth understanding of the market, allowing us to provide you with the best quality services and most competitive products.

Our products cover a variety of wedding and party supplies, including wedding table decoration, floral decoration, lighting effects, and stage backdrops. We are committed to creating a unique experience for every wedding, leaving new couples and their family and friends with unforgettable memories.

In our studio, we attach great importance to details and quality. Each of our products undergoes strict screening and testing to ensure that they meet our high standards. We believe that only by making every product with heart can we win the trust and praise of customers.

Our mission is to create dream weddings for each couple through design. We look forward to working with you to create beautiful memories together.
